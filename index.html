What we called "New things" are not about inventing things that you naver saw. Every innovation is derived from an organic combination of solutions that already exist. The architecture and construction industry is no exception.
I'm an architect and a programmer at the same time. I develop tools for architectural design, use mathematical techniques to solve architectural problems, studying and applying generative design methods that are at the forefront of the field.
I'm very sure that computational design will soon bring a new trend and I'm waiting for that time. I'm running toward that goal with a vision to create fine buildings with codes, bring the design from virtual to reality, that is, to realize the metaverse of the architectural industry.
